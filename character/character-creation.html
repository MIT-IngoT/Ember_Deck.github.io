CHARACTER CREATION

Character Creation

  1. Start with a concept: who is this character, and what do they do in the world?
  2. Pick an Oath: this sets your core Prisms (MIGHT, GUILE, INSIGHT) and starting deck identity.
  3. Build your starting deck: use the listed Oath deck, then apply any allowed starter options, shards, or oath-specific choices. (OATH_OF_THE_PYRE OATH_OF_THE_CONDUIT OATH_OF_THE_MISTWALKER)
  4. Choose your CHARGE.
  5. Optional: add personality using Quirks from the TALK_THE_TALK for inspiration, especially if you want extra flavour beyond the mechanics.
  6. Write a short backstory for the GM, in collaboration with the other players, explaining how you're bonded, unless it is pre-specified in the hook.
  7. Oprtional: If you're playing a One-shot, or a shorter campaign, it is likely you will be using a 3-2-1 Prism distribution instead of the standard 2-1-0 +1.

Example Characters

Forrest Perry

OATH_OF_THE_PYRE
Passive: MEND MIGHT VITALS at combat end.
CHARGE: Sacrifice up to MIGHT VITALS, gain as much BRACE, and 2 X MOMENTUM
Prism Scores: 2 GUILE, 1 INSIGHT, 3 MIGHT.
In‑World Occupation
Forrest works as an Ember mortician with Controlled Release Services, preparing the unstable and terminal for safe venting and disposal in city wards.
Backstory
He survived a ward failure that should have turned the whole building into a scorching crater, calmly moving patients into improvised cooling rigs and keeping collapse to a minimum while others panicked. Since then, commanders quietly pass his name along whenever a district needs someone who will stay in the room until the dust settles.
Role in the Hearth
At the table, Forrest is the Hearth’s self‑sacrificing stabilizer, spending VITALS to bulk up BRACE and stock MOMENTUM, then patching the group with MEND at combat end. In roleplay, he is the one who assumes the ugly burdens, stepping into danger with the expectation that his Hearth will turn his spent health and quiet reliability into a lasting advantage.

Jessie Snow

OATH_OF_THE_PYRE
Passive: At the end of each combat round, gain +1 MOMENTUM.
CHARGE: STRIKE with MOMENTUM multiplier (DMG = STRIKE X MOMENTUM, instead of STRIKE + MOMENTUM).
Prism Scores: 2 GUILE, 1 INSIGHT, 3 MIGHT.
In‑World Occupation
Jessie serves as a trench courier, sprinting between bunkers and battlelines to relay orders and mark shifting lines.
Backstory
On one infamous night they ran the trench's length three times, carrying corrections that stopped an allied regiment from being shelled by its own compatriots, and each run was somehow faster than the last.
Role in the Hearth
Mechanically, Jessie is a tempo engine who converts steady, reliable gain of MOMENTUM into a single overwhelming CHARGE that multiplies STRIKE instead of merely adding to it. In roleplay, Jessie sets the rhythm of fights, constantly urging the Hearth to keep the sequence going so that when they finally call for their big move, everyone has already helped load that decisive hit

Arthur Sparks

OATH_OF_THE_CONDUIT
Passive: Your IGNITE effects do not decay.
CHARGE: IGNITE INSIGHT Distribute the stacks as you wish. Already IGNITEd enemies take their total IGNITE stacks as DMG immediately (Does not remove stacks).
Prism Scores: 1 GUILE, 3 INSIGHT, 2 MIGHT.
In‑World Occupation
Lantern grid technician in a dense city, maintaining the web of ward‑lights that keep the Grey Mist thin in alleys and lower city levels.
Backstory
Arthur once stabilized an entire district’s lantern network by chaining his own resonance through faulty cables, turning himself into a continuous generator that stopped Hollows from drifting in. Once he lights something, he is obsessed with keeping that flame “on spec” instead of letting it burn out.
Role in the Hearth
Mechanically, Arthur leans on INSIGHT by making IGNITE effects persistent and then redistributing those stacks with his CHARGE, including sudden bursts of damage to enemies that are already burning. In roleplay, he treats enemies and hazards like nodes in a circuit, asking his Hearth to tag, corral, and revisit targets so his lingering fire can keep compounding while the others manoeuvre around his planned burn pattern.

Clyde Meyer

OATH_OF_THE_CONDUIT
Passive: Leftover GUARD is halved at turn end, instead of vanishing completely.
CHARGE: STRIKE = Missing VITALS
Prism Scores: 2 GUILE, 3 INSIGHT, 1 MIGHT.
In‑World Occupation
Clyde works as a private investigator in the city, taking on missing persons cases, quiet blackmail jobs, and disputes that the officials would rather avoid.
Backstory
He made his reputation by following cases everyone else had already written off, especially the ones that led through sealed alleys, and neighbourhoods where people preferred not to be seen answering questions. What set him apart was not charm or violence, but stubborn persistence and a habit of noticing what others walked right past.
Role in the Hearth
Clyde’s mechanics reward taking hits in a controlled way, as his passive lets leftover GUARD persist and his CHARGE turns missing VITALS into a devastating STRIKE. In roleplay, he invites the Hearth to stand behind him when they cannot afford to fall, making it clear that every point of damage he soaks now will come back later when he finally swings with everything he has lost

Kay Freeman

OATH_OF_THE_MISTWALKER
Passive: Discard the first negative STATUS drawn (by You) each turn without triggering its effects.
CHARGE: Reduce the cost of all cards currently dealt to 1 this turn.
Prism Scores: 3 GUILE, 2 INSIGHT, 1 MIGHT.
In‑World Occupation
Kay is a corridor smuggler who specializes in leading groups through half‑lit, Mist‑choked routes that official patrols consider too unstable to risk.
Backstory
She earned her name by always finding a way through. When one path was sealed, she came back with two more, and when the straight route failed, she knew the side door, the roof line, the drain passage, or the gap between buildings that nobody else had thought to try. That instinct for alternate routes and urban escape lines is what makes her great at slipping through the city, unstoppable.
Role in the Hearth
Kay’s toolkit leans on GUILE, discarding the first negative STATUS they draw each turn and using CHARGE to slash the ENERGY cost of cards currently in play down for a turn. In roleplay, they function as the Hearth’s mental filter and opportunist, steering the group away from psychic clutter while turning a single good turn of draws into a sudden storm of cheap, coordinated plays.

Pam Walker

OATH_OF_THE_MISTWALKER
Passive: Dealing unblocked HP damage grants +1 CHARGE.
CHARGE: Stun an enemy. (Pause their Intent)
Prism Scores: 3 GUILE, 1 INSIGHT, 2 MIGHT.
In‑World Occupation
Pam is an alley enforcer who does quiet contract work for locals, tracking down volatile Embers, Cinders, and predators that threaten her crowded streets.
Backstory
She first made a name cleaning up after a failed ritual in a tenement block, hunting the surviving participants through stairwells and courtyards before their instability turned into a full collapse incident. To the people who live there, she is not an assassin or a saviour but something just morally grey enough in the middle of the two.
Role in the Hearth
Pam’s mechanics reward precise, unblocked aggression, gaining CHARGE whenever she deals HP damage that gets through defenses and spending it to stun enemies, pausing their Intent. In roleplay, she serves as the Hearth’s opener and control piece, using quick, decisive strikes to set the pace of a fight and create windows where the others can commit to high‑risk plays while key threats are locked in place.
